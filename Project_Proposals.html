FinTracker

FinTracker — Your Smart Spending Coach

Idea
A web application that helps users track expenses and set financial goals, offering personalized advice based on their spending habits.
Main Feature
Expense analysis by category and automatic recommendations (e.g.: “You spend €80 a month on coffee — here’s where you can save.”)
Problem & Target Audience
Many young people lose track of small recurring expenses and don’t have an easy plan to reach short-term goals (travel, equipment, subscriptions). Target audience: students, young professionals, and budget-conscious users.
Core Features (MVP)
  • Account creation & secure login (JWT + password hashing).
  • Bank-like ledger: add expenses & incomes manually; categorize transactions.
  • Simple goals: set a target and track progress (e.g.: "Save €200 for new boots").
  • Dashboard: monthly summary, category pie chart, top recurring spenders.
  • Transaction list with search, filters, and CRUD operations.
  • Owner/admin dashboard: manage users, flag suspicious data, statistics.
FitBattle

FitBattle — Challenge Your Friends

Idea
A social fitness app where users set personal goals (e.g.: “Run 10 km per week”) and compete with friends in challenges.
Main Feature
Rankings, achievements, and integration with fitness trackers (e.g.: "You beat your friend John on this distance").
Problem & Target Audience
Many people start fitness routines but lose motivation. Gamification and social accountability help. Target audience: amateur athletes, gym-goers, friends groups, and university sports teams.
Core Features (MVP)
  • User accounts & profile (strength levels, preferred sports).
  • Create or join challenges (time-based or goal-based: e.g.: "Run 20 km in 2 weeks").
  • Log activities manually or via CSV upload; optional Strava/Google Fit sync later.
  • Leaderboard & progress feed; comments and emojis for social motivation.
  • Admin dashboard: manage challenges, moderate content, export stats.
